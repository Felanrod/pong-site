Pong

The Cutting Edge of Entertainment

Have you always wanted to play ping-pong, but couldn't becasue you don't have any friends?

Now you can!

The fine folks at have created a masterpiece that will put the fun back in Your life must be boring, have some fun!

With their revolutionary technology, the brand new interactive electronic entertainment company, Atari Inc. has created an arcade hit that is sweeping the continent! The ping–pong simulator allows you to direct a virtual paddle, with a radial dial, up and down a screen while you try to keep the ball (also virtual) from going past your paddle. I know what you're thinking: Joel, how are you so good looking and smart? That's one of life's great mysteries, dear reader. What you should be asking is: How is it possible to play by yourself if no one is controlling the other paddle? That's what the geniuses, not unlike myself, at Atari Inc. have figured out. For single player games an artificial intelligence, or AI, has been programmed into the system. In a sense it's as if the machine has a mind of its own, with its ability to move the paddle and block the incoming ball. If machines can competitively go aginst a human at ping–pong, imagine what else they can do…

Already a hit in the ten most important states, and three most important provinces, Pong is ready to become a staple of your daily life.
